This is my collection

Welcome to the exhibition of my car collection. I have long been fascinated by Muscle Cars, iconic American vehicles known for their power and robust design. In addition to these classics, my collection includes Teslas, as my passion began with one of these innovative electric vehicles. I also have loose cars gifted by friends and other well-crafted pieces that have caught my eye. I hope you enjoy the tour!
